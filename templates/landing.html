Your Data, Your Rules

DataGrabber is designed with security and flexibility in mind

Total Privacy & Security

Your confidential documents never leave your PC. All data processing happens locally using your machine's resources, ensuring maximum privacy and security. No cloud uploads, no data breaches, no unauthorized access - your sensitive information stays completely under your control.

Smart Template Builder

Define exactly what you want to extract with our intuitive template system. Create custom field mappings for your specific document types, then reuse them across hundreds of similar documents. Perfect for invoices, contracts, receipts, forms, and any repetitive document processing.

Real-Time Editing & Control

Fine-tune your results instantly with our built-in editor. Modify templates on-the-fly, correct any extraction errors, and adjust field mappings without starting over. Your workflow adapts to you, not the other way around.

Universal Format Support

From PDFs to images (JPG, PNG), DataGrabber handles various formats with state-of-the-art AI and OCR technology. Whether it's scanned documents, digital PDFs, or photos taken with your phone, our system extracts text and data with remarkable accuracy.

High-Volume Processing

Process hundreds of documents in minutes, not hours. Upload entire folders and let DataGrabber work through them automatically using your saved templates. Scale from single documents to enterprise-level batch processing with ease.

Export & Integration Ready

All extracted data is organized in editable tables that you can export to CSV, Excel, or integrate with your existing systems. Clean, structured data that's ready for analysis, reporting, or import into databases and business applications.
